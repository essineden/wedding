Michael Joseph Pelone

Michael/ Majo/ My elder bro/ My best friend is a person to keep. We may be miles apart now but I know we always and forever have each other's back. He's that person who will give you options and light in your decision making, someone reliable and won't leave you in bad times. He has always been and forever will be one of the few people I completely trust, my confidante. He's there to build your confidence when you start doubting yourself, support you in aidless times - the eldest brother that I never had. And right now, we may not talk that much because of different and busy schedules but it's undoubtedly that I'm one of the happiest to finally see him happy and in love with the one he truly deserves. From that childish, gamer, computer geek Majo to a mature, well rounded and loving husband. I couldn't be any prouder. Here's to a brighter future with your lovely wife, Cai. Cheers!!!
